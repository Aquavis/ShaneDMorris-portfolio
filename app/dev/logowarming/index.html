Help keep Rochester cool

From sweltering summers to flash floods, our region is already feeling the effects of climate change. So at the Rochester People's Climate Coalition, we're working to reduce local carbon emissions by helping our city transition to all-electric energy. But we need your help.

Raise money and awareness for a more sustainable city by taking the #LogoWarming Challenge.
